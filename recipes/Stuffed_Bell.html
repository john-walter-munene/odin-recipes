Stuffed Bell

Description

This is a complete guide to making the Stuffed Bell Meal.

Ingridients

  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Cooking oil
  • An egg

Steps

  1. Preheat pan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square pan with cooking oil.
  2. Fry the pepper and onions together for 3 minutes.
  3. Add the egg and stir till mixture is dry.
