African Kuku

Description

This is a complete guide to making the African Kuku.

Ingridients

  • tbsp ginger finely minced
  • 1 tbsp garlic finely minced
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp red chili powder optional
  • 1 tsp coriander powder (ground cilantro)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder (ground cumin)

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken by filling a large pot or bowl with water and squeezing in the juice of half a lemon. Alternatively, you can clean the chicken by patting it dry using a paper towel. Next, cut the chicken to servable pieces, trim off excess fat from the chicken, and make diagonal slits on the surface to allow the marinade to penetrate through. Transfer the prepared chicken pieces to a bowl.
  2. To the bowl with the chicken pieces, add lemon juice, minced garlic, ginger, black pepper, red chili powder, coriander, cumin, turmeric and salt. Stir in the olive oil then give it a good mix to combine.
  3. Cover, place in the refrigerator and allow to marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight for best results
